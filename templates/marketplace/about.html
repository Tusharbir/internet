About UniTrade

About UniTrade

UniTrade is a simple and secure platform where students can buy and sell items within their university community.

Save money, reduce waste, and connect with other students.

What makes UniTrade useful?
  • Campus-focused buying and selling
  • Simple tools for posting and browsing
  • Clear item photos and details
  • Easy account and activity tracking

Why We Built This

Students often need affordable textbooks, electronics, furniture, and everyday items, but large marketplaces can feel crowded and difficult to trust.

UniTrade was created to give students a safer and simpler place to buy and sell within their own campus community. The goal is to make student life more affordable while helping useful items stay in circulation instead of going to waste.

Built for campus life

UniTrade keeps the experience focused on student needs, trusted connections, and practical everyday buying and selling.

What You Can Do

Post items for sale
Browse items from other students
Contact sellers easily
Save favorite items
Track your activity
Upload images for better listings

Key Features

Simple tools that make buying and selling easier for students.

Easy sign up and login

Create an account, sign in quickly, and manage your marketplace activity in one place.

Secure user access

Important actions are available only to the right users, helping keep the platform organized and safer.

Smart search and filters

Find listings faster using search, categories, conditions, and price filters.

Upload photos for listings

Add clear pictures so buyers can better understand what you are selling.

Personal dashboard

Manage drafts, active listings, sold items, and conversations from one simple dashboard.

Recently viewed tracking

Revisit items you have looked at before without needing to search for them again.
