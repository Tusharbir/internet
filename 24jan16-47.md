## 3) Models Implemented
`marketplace/models.py`
- **User** extends `AbstractUser` with `university_email`, `student_id`
- **Category**: name + slug
- **Item**:
  - core fields: title, description, price, condition, category
  - advanced fields: status (draft/published/sold), location, negotiable
- **ItemImage**: multi-image upload stored in `MEDIA_ROOT/items/`
- **Message**: buyer/seller messages
- **Favorite**: wishlist (unique per user/item)
- **Report**: moderation reports (unique per user/item)
